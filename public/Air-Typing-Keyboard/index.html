Welcome to AirType AI

Type in mid-air using hand gestures. No keyboard needed.

Powered by advanced computer vision and AI-driven predictions, AirType transforms your webcam into a futuristic typing interface.

Camera Access

We need your camera to track hand movements.

1 Click "Allow" when your browser asks for camera permission
2 Ensure your hands are visible and well-lit
3 Keep about arm's length from the camera

🔒 Video is processed locally. Nothing is sent to any server.
