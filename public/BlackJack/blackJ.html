BLACK JACK

How To Play

  • Goal: Reach 21 without exceeding it
  • Hit → Draw another card
  • Stand → Stop drawing; dealer plays
  • Dealer draws until score ≥ 17
  • Closest to 21 wins — ties are a Draw
  • Bust → Score > 21 is an instant loss
