About ChronoSphere

ChronoSphere is a premium history explorer built with vanilla JavaScript and local JSON data. It lets you browse major events across eras, filter by category, and explore a timeline presented in a sleek dark-glass interface.

Use the search and filters to discover stories from ancient civilizations, scientific breakthroughs, cultural milestones, and the future of human progress.

Every event is rendered client-side for a smooth experience, with responsive design and accessible interactions for modern browsers.
