Budget Planning Tips

Make the most of your event budget

📅 Book Early, Save More

Booking 3+ months in advance can save up to 20% on overall costs.

🍽️ Opt for a Set Menu

A fixed catering menu is always more cost-effective than unlimited buffet options.

🌸 Seasonal Flowers

Using in-season flowers for decoration reduces floral costs by 30–40%.

📦 Bundle Packages

Our complete packages bundle all services at a discounted rate compared to booking individually.

💡 Off-Peak Dates

Hosting on weekdays or non-peak months (Feb–Apr) can significantly lower venue and vendor costs.

Our team offers free consultation to optimise your event for maximum value within your budget.
