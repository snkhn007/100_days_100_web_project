    📐 Formula Reference

    Equal credits per semester:

    Required Avg SGPA = ((Target CGPA × Total Semesters) − (Current CGPA × Completed Semesters)) ÷ Remaining Semesters

    Example: CGPA 7.2, 4 completed, target 8.5, 4 remaining (8 total)

    = ((8.5 × 8) − (7.2 × 4)) ÷ 4 = (68 − 28.8) ÷ 4 = 9.8
