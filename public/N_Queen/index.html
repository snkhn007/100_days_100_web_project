N-Queens Game

Place N queens safely without them attacking each other!

📖 How to Play & Game Rules

What is the N-Queens problem?
Place N chess queens on an N×N board so that no two queens threaten each other.

How queens move:

  • A queen attacks every square in its row, column, and both diagonals.

Your goal: Place exactly N queens so none of them can attack another.

Beginner tips:

  • Start with N = 4 or 5 — smaller boards are easier to solve.
  • Hover over a square to preview which cells it threatens (highlighted in orange).
  • Click a placed queen again to remove it.
