About GO-GREEN

Who we are, what drives us, and why the planet comes first.

Our Story

GO-GREEN was born from a simple conviction: every person, household, and business has the power to make choices that protect the earth. Since 2018 we have been turning that conviction into action — building awareness, tools, and community around sustainable living.

Where We Started

What began as a small neighbourhood clean-up initiative in 2018 has grown into a nationwide network of volunteers, educators, and eco-innovators. Our founders believed that lasting change starts locally — one street, one school, one family at a time.

Over the years we expanded our reach through digital platforms, partnering with local governments and NGOs to multiply impact without multiplying waste.
