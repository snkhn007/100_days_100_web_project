Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No — our first conversation is always free. We want to understand your needs before recommending any paid service.

Do you work outside Tamil Nadu?

Yes. Workshops and consulting are available nationwide in-person and online. Reforestation drives are currently Tamil Nadu-focused.

How do I volunteer?

Fill in the contact form above, select "Partnership / Sponsorship", and mention you'd like to volunteer. We'll add you to our next onboarding batch.

Can schools get free workshops?

Government schools and registered non-profits receive a 50 % discount automatically, and in many cases full sponsorship through our CSR partners.
