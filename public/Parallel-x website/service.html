What We Offer

From individual households to large enterprises, our services are designed to meet you exactly where you are on your sustainability journey — and take you further.

Green Audits

Our certified auditors assess your home or office for energy waste, water usage, and carbon output. You receive a detailed report with prioritised, cost-effective recommendations tailored to your situation.

  • Full energy consumption analysis
  • Water and waste audit
  • Personalised action roadmap
  • 12-month follow-up check-in

Renewable Energy Consulting

Navigating solar, wind, and battery storage options can be overwhelming. Our experts cut through the noise and guide you to the right renewable solution for your budget and location.

  • Solar panel feasibility study
  • Grid vs off-grid comparison
  • Vendor-neutral installer referrals
  • Subsidy and incentive mapping

School & Corporate Workshops

Engaging, hands-on sessions that build lasting eco-habits in students and employees. From climate science fundamentals to zero-waste office challenges — we make learning stick.

  • Age-appropriate curriculum design
  • Interactive waste-sorting activities
  • Corporate carbon-footprint challenges
  • Certificate of Participation for attendees

Community Reforestation

Join our organised tree-planting drives or sponsor a grove in your name. Every sapling is tracked via our digital dashboard so you can watch your impact grow in real time.

  • Native species selection
  • GPS-tagged planting records
  • Sponsor-a-tree certificates
  • Annual growth photo reports
