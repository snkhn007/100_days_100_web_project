# 💸 SpendSense – Smart Expense Tracker

![SpendSense Banner](favicon.png)

> A modern, elegant, and fully client-side expense tracking application built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.

SpendSense helps users monitor their income, expenses, savings, and spending habits with beautiful visual insights including charts, statistics, and category breakdowns.

---

## ✨ Features

### 💰 Expense & Income Management

* Add income and expense transactions.
* Select categories such as:

  * 💼 Salary
  * 🍔 Food
  * 🚗 Transport
  * 🛍️ Shopping
  * 💡 Bills
  * ❤️ Health
  * 📦 Other
* Add custom descriptions and dates.
* Delete individual transactions.
* Clear all transaction history.

---

## 📊 Financial Dashboard

### Summary Cards

* Total Income
* Total Expenses
* Net Balance with profit/loss indication

### Charts & Analytics

* 📈 Monthly Income vs Expense bar chart (last 6 months)
* 🍩 Expense category breakdown donut chart
* ⚡ Quick financial statistics:

  * Biggest expense
  * Average expense
  * Total entries
  * Savings rate

---

## 🎨 Modern User Interface

* Dark futuristic theme.
* Smooth animations and transitions.
* Responsive layout.
* Custom typography using **Syne** and **DM Mono** fonts.
* Interactive buttons and toast notifications.

---

## 💾 Data Storage

SpendSense uses the browser's **Local Storage** API to save transaction data.

Your data:

* ✔ Stays on your device.
* ✔ Persists after page refresh.
* ✔ Requires no account or login.
* ✔ Is never sent to a server.
