☕ Virtual Rain Cafe Guide

Welcome to your cozy workspace! Here are some features you can enjoy:

  • Soundboard Mixer: Mix ambient rain, crackling fireplace, wind, and bustling cafe sounds to create your ideal audio background.
  • Lo-Fi Player: Play smooth chill beats with a spinning vinyl. Adjust the seek bar or skip tracks at will.
  • Canvas Rain: Watch realistic raindrop animations in the background, which change angle, speed, and thickness based on your selected theme and rain intensity!
  • Cozy Timer: Keep track of your Pomodoro work cycles. Focus for 25 mins, then take a short "sip" break.
  • Order Checklist & Notes: Add cafe order items as tasks. Notes auto-save in your browser, and writing them with "Keyboard Sound" enabled generates mechanical keyboard typing clicks!
  • Quick Toggles: Press the dimmer lightbulb in the header to dim the screen, or use the eye icon to hide the dashboard UI entirely and just enjoy the raining environment.
