Know More About Indian Flag

Saffron

The saffron color represents courage, sacrifice, and the spirit of strength.

White

The white stripe symbolizes peace, truth, and honesty.

Green

The green color represents growth, prosperity, and harmony with nature.

Ashoka Chakra

The Ashoka Chakra stands for progress, dharma, and continuous movement in life.
