How to Play

Slide tiles into the empty space to arrange them in order (1 to n²-1).

  • Click or tap adjacent tiles
  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Every New Game gives a fresh solvable puzzle
Help & Tips

Tip: Solve smaller boards first. Watch the empty space movement.

Image mode works best with high-contrast photos.

Undo is limited to last 10 moves.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Arrow Keys or WASD — Move blank space

N — New Game

R — Restart

P — Pause / Resume

H — Hint

U — Undo
